Our Story

Sasi cakes is a bakery shop run by three childhood friends, It all started as a small shop in a local town called Bakau, but now we have three shops in different locations.We offer you to experience true pleasure, We make cakes, cupcakes, cookies and more.Our mission is to make people happy. Making delicious cakes, having relaxing chairs in a smoking free environment and keeping our prices reasonable are all different ways to achieve this goal
